How Validation Works

The system traverses the entire blockchain and performs two critical checks on every block:

  • Data Integrity: Recalculates the SHA-256 hash of the block's current data and compares it to the stored hash.
  • Chain Continuity: Verifies that the block's `previous_hash` exactly matches the `current_hash` of the preceding block.

Academic Demonstration

This feature allows you to simulate a cyberattack by intentionally modifying data in the blockchain without recalculating the hashes. This will break the cryptographic chain and demonstrate the tamper-detection capabilities.
